Mirchi Masala offers a variety of food items including dessert. Browse our items and satisy your taste buds!

Dal Makhani: Black lentils cooked with butter and cream and simmered on low heat for that unique flavor!

Aloo Matar: spiced creamy tomato based sauce cooked with garlic, ginger, onion, tomatoes, cilantro, and peas.

Biryani: Rice cooked with either lamb, chicken, fish, or vegetables along with spices like saffron in a rich gravy.

Chicken Vandaloo: meatballs zapped with ginger, garlic and garam masala and arranged on fluffy brown rice.

Garlic Naan: An Indian flatbread made on the stovetop and garnished with garlic and cilantro.

Gulab Jamun: dessert consisting of delicious doughnuts made of powdered milk that are soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup.

Matar Paneer: Sweet creamy gravy containing cottage chesse and peas.
